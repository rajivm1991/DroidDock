DroidDock v0.2.2

Release Notes • January 17, 2026

What's New in v0.2.2

Manual Dark Mode Toggle

New dark mode toggle in settings lets you manually switch between light and dark themes. Your preference is saved and persists across sessions. No longer auto-switches based on system theme.

Improved App Icon

Fixed app icon padding for better consistency in macOS Cmd+Tab app switcher and Dock.

Progress Display Fixes

Fixed download and upload progress indicators to correctly show transfer status and success messages.
